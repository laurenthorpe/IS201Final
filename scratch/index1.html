Top Places to Visit in Frankfurt

  1. Römerberg
    • Historic town square
    • Beautiful medieval architecture
    • Great for sightseeing and photos
  2. Palmengarten
    • Large botanical garden
    • Indoor and outdoor plants
    • Perfect for a peaceful walk
  3. Städel Museum
    • Art museum with collections from the Renaissance to contemporary art
    • Great for art lovers
  4. Main Tower
    • Panoramic views of the city from the observation deck
    • Skyscraper located in central Frankfurt
  5. Goethe House
    • Birthplace of Johann Wolfgang von Goethe
    • Learn about the famous German writer
